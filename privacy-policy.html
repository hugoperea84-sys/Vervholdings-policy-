Privacy Policy

Clarity is a private internal finance tool used exclusively by authorized personnel. It is not intended for public or consumer use.

Data Access and Usage

Clarity connects to QuickBooks Online using Intuit OAuth 2.0 with explicit user authorization. It accesses only the data required for internal reporting and reconciliation, including account balances, transactions, vendors, and invoices.

No other data is collected. Access is read-only and strictly limited to authorized personnel.

Data Sharing

No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties. All data is stored securely and is accessible only to authorized users.

Security
